# Fashion Industry Domain Knowledge

Comprehensive reference for fashion industry context, calendars, and terminology. This skill enhances Claude's understanding for fashion-specific queries across data analysis, content creation, and market research.

## When This Skill Applies

Activate this knowledge when:
- Analyzing fashion industry data (job postings, marketplace, editorial)

- Writing fashion news, trend reports, or market analysis
- Interpreting seasonal patterns in fashion data

### Key Terminology Quick Reference

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| SS / S/S | Spring/Summer collection |
| AW / A/W | Autumn/Winter collection (also FW / F/W in US) |
| RTW | Ready-to-wear (pret-a-porter) |
| Haute Couture | Custom-fitted high fashion (regulated term in France) |
| Pre-fall / Resort / Cruise | Inter-season collections |
| Look / Exit | Single outfit shown on runway |
| Capsule | Small, cohesive collection |
| Drop | Product release (especially streetwear) |

## Using This Knowledge

### For Data Analysis

When analyzing fashion industry data:
- Consider **seasonality**: Job postings spike before fashion weeks; retail data follows seasonal buying cycles
- Account for **lead times**: Brands plan 6-18 months ahead; trade fair attendance predicts upcoming trends
- Recognize **regional differences**: Fashion weeks drive different markets; German market follows Berlin, US follows NYFW

### For Content Creation

When creating fashion content:
- Align topics with the **fashion calendar**: Cover upcoming events, not past ones
- Use **correct terminology**: SS26 means Spring/Summer 2026; AW25 is Autumn/Winter 2025
- Understand **content types**: News (immediate), trend reports (analytical), features (evergreen)

### For Market Research

When researching fashion markets:
- **Segment by category**: Luxury, fast fashion, sustainable, sportswear have different dynamics
- **Track trade fair attendance**: Signals sourcing trends and material innovations
- **Monitor fashion week coverage**: Indicates brand investment and market positioning
