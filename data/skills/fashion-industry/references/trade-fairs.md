# Fashion Trade Fair Calendar

Comprehensive schedule of major B2B fashion trade fairs for textiles, materials, sourcing, and finished goods. Trade fairs are key for business intelligence, sourcing trends, and industry networking.

## Textile and Materials Trade Fairs

### Premiere Vision Paris

The world's leading fabric and materials trade fair.

| Edition | Typical Dates | Location |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Spring/Summer sourcing | February | Paris Nord Villepinte |
| Autumn/Winter sourcing | September | Paris Nord Villepinte |

**Focus areas:**
- Fabrics and textiles (yarns, wovens, knits)
- Leather and fur
- Designs and prints
- Accessories and components
- Manufacturing solutions

**Key zones:**
- Premiere Vision Fabrics (main textile hall)
- Premiere Vision Leather (leather and skins)
- Premiere Vision Manufacturing (production partners)
- Premiere Vision Designs (print and pattern)
- Premiere Vision Accessories (trims, buttons, zippers)

**Content opportunities:**
- Trend forecasting reports (colors, materials, textures)
- Sustainability innovations
- Exhibitor interviews
- Sourcing guides
- Market analysis

## Trade Fair Coverage Planning

### Pre-Event (1-2 weeks before)
- What to expect guides
- Exhibitor previews
- Trend predictions
- Buyer attendance guides

### During Event (each day)
- Hall-by-hall coverage
- Exhibitor spotlights
- Innovation highlights
- Sustainability features
- Expert interviews

### Post-Event (1 week after)
- Trend summary reports
- Key takeaways
- Market implications
- Next event preview
